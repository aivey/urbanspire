How it Works
Connecting newcomers and locals one class at a time

Who are the teachers?

UrbanSpire teachers are newcomers who wish to teach a class about his/her home culture. They are not professionals, just neighbors who wish to share a little bit about their culture with the rest of their community in a neighbor-to-neighbor context.

Who are the learners?

UrbanSpire learners are both newcomers and locals who wish to learn more about a cultural activity. Participating in classes helps a person become a more global citizen while facilitating interactions with other neighbors in the community.

How do I learn?

Newcomers and locals can learn.

 Browse the catalog of available classes taught by your fellow neighbors. Search by cultural origin, activity, and/or distance.


 Sign up for a class that interests you and that fits with your schedule.


 Meet your neighbor(s) and learn about their culture!


How do I teach?

Only newcomers can teach.

 Create a class with a description, location, and your available times.


 Wait until your class has participants. You will receive confirmation on your class's enrollment.


 Meet your neighbor(s) and teach them about your home culture!
