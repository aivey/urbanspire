Meet the Team
From Stanford University

UrbanSpire emerged from a Stanford class called CS194H: User Interface Design Project. We were inspired by the cultural wealth of the communities around us, and intrigued by the challenge of allowing community members to tap into those resources in a fun and convenient manner so as to create stronger cross-cultural bonds. We thus decided to develop a learning and teaching platform that allows for newcomers to feel more comfortable in their new environments.
