About Us


Context

UrbanSpire first came to life in a Fall 2015 class offering of CS247:HCI Design Studio at Stanford University. The theme of the class was urban computing, and suggestions for projects spanned environmental concerns and eco-sustainability, helping the disabled navigate urban spaces, and making urban spaces more pet-friendly.
