What is the purpose of this site?

The purpose of this site is to help you discover and make the most of the cultural wealth present in your community. The people that we cross on the streets and in the elevator on a daily basis often hail from infinitely varied regions, and consequently have a panoply of skills and experiences that we could (until now) only dream of: think Chinese calligraphy, Australian slang, Javanese sculpture, Turkish belly-dancing…

At the same time, these community members are likely not as familiar with your part of the world as you are.

UrbanSpire is the possibility to learn from and teach each other these culturally-specific skills in a relaxed, safe and convenient manner. Choose your preferred activity, teacher and time slots, and you are all set to embark upon your journey of cultural discovery!

How do I know whether I am a “newcomer” or a “community member”?

It depends entirely on how you feel, or what you identify with!

How does the search work?

Our search relies on three categories: activity class (e.g. cooking, dancing, art), cultural origin (e.g. Africa, Asia, Europe) and distance from where you are (5 miles, 10 miles…) By combining these different factors, you can find the cultural class that you have always wanted to take!

And if it doesn’t exist yet… it means it is time for you to create it!
